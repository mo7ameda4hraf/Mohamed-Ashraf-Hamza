Quick Insurance :

Quick Insurance is a prototype web application for a digital insurance company, designed to provide an easy-to-use platform for quickly obtaining insurance offers and prices.

Key Features :

Data entry form to request a quote including (ID/Iqama number, serial number, date of birth, and insurance start date).

Dedicated sections introducing the company and its core values (Trust – Quality – Innovation – Professionalism).

A section highlighting why customers should choose Quick Insurance (Customized solutions – Ease of use – Unique experience).

Our Services” section covering different insurance types (Medical, Travel, Savings & Protection, Professional Liability, etc.).

Project Objective :

Simulate the experience of a digital insurance company, allowing customers to request quotes online with ease.

Showcase the ability to design a professional and scalable user interface

Tools :

Html, Css, Javascript, bootstrap, Aos, Slick .
